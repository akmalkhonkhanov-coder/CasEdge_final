The Case Interview

What top firms actually test

A case interview is a live business problem you solve out loud with the interviewer. It's how McKinsey, BCG, and Bain decide who gets the offer — and it rewards structure, sharp math, and clear communication over memorized answers.

Structure
Break an open-ended problem into clean, MECE buckets — and lead with your hypothesis, not a generic framework.
Quant
Run multi-step business math under pressure — breakeven, margins, market sizing — and tie every number back to the decision.
Synthesis
Deliver an answer-first recommendation with the conclusion up front, supporting data, and the risks that come with it.

Frequently asked questions

What is a case interview?
A case interview is a live business problem you solve out loud with an interviewer. It's the core of how McKinsey, BCG, and Bain assess candidates — testing structure, quantitative skill, business judgment, and communication.
What's the difference between cases and drills?
A full case is a complete ~40-minute simulation — structure, math, exhibits, and a final recommendation. Drills isolate a single skill (math, structuring, synthesis, and more) into a fast 5-rep series so you can build that one muscle quickly.
How is the feedback different from other tools?
Most tools hand you a sample answer to compare against. CasEdge coaches the way a partner would — it pushes back on weak logic, holds the numbers firm under pressure, and shows you how to think, not just what the answer was.
Which firms and case types are covered?
Cases follow real MBB patterns across firms (McKinsey, BCG, Bain) and types — profitability, market entry, M&A, pricing, market sizing, and more. Drills cover eight distinct skills tested in real interviews.
What languages are supported?
The platform is expanding to English, Russian, and Spanish — with the same case difficulty and depth across every language.
Is my progress saved?
Yes — your progress is currently saved on your device. Account-based sign-in with cloud sync across devices is coming soon.
