Help

Frequently asked questions

What is a case interview?
A case interview is a live business problem you solve out loud with an interviewer. It's the core of how McKinsey, BCG, and Bain assess candidates - testing structure, quantitative skill, business judgment, and communication.
What's the difference between cases and drills?
A full case is a complete ~40-minute simulation - structure, math, exhibits, and a final recommendation. Drills isolate a single skill (math, structuring, synthesis, and more) into a fast 5-rep series so you can build that one muscle quickly.
How is the feedback different from other tools?
Most tools hand you a sample answer to compare against. CasEdge coaches the way a partner would - it pushes back on weak logic, holds the numbers firm under pressure, and shows you how to think, not just what the answer was.
Which firms and case types are covered?
Cases follow real MBB patterns across firms (McKinsey, BCG, Bain) and types - profitability, market entry, M&A, pricing, market sizing, and more. Drills cover seven distinct skills tested in real interviews.
What languages are supported?
The platform supports English and Russian - with the same case difficulty, depth, and rigor across both languages.
Is my progress saved?
Yes - your progress is saved on your device, and account-based cloud sync keeps it consistent across all your devices.
